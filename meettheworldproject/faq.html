Frequently Asked Questions

Below you will find the answers to the most commonly asked questions about traveling with us. Please use the contact us page if you don't find the answers you are looking for.

Why should I plan my vacation to a natural destination?

Being in nature has been proven to benefit a person's physical and mental health. Traveling to a natural destination assure you a break from stress and gives you a breath of fresh air. Also, it motivates you to do physical activity kind of life, can improve your mood, and help you feel more energized. Furthermore, it gives you the most exciting feeling of discovering and admiring places that are often overlooked.

Why Meet The World only works with natural destinations?

Meet the world would like to spread love and respect to our mother nature by committing to give the most exciting adventures you can only find in natural destinations safely.

How do I know I will be safe in a natural destination?

Meet the world works with professional local tourist guides. Of course, there are always a set of rules that you need to follow, but these rules do not limitate you from exploring the natural areas. All groups have at least one guide, but if the group is larger than 6, the group will have two guides at all times while you enjoy nature.

What are the main requirements to travel to a natural destination?

Be prepared to walk. Our routes are designed to travel a portion by car, but there is always a portion to be walked. Depending on your destination, we will provide a list of all items you need to bring.

How do I know I can trust Meet The world: Travel Agency?

Meet The World Agency has been around for fifteen years. During this period we had fun. We have created a family with members worldwide who are committed to your safety while you enjoy nature.
