Get to know me

My name is Nayeli Zapata Reyes. I was born and raised in Pinos Zacatecas, Mexico. I moved to the USA in May of 2009.
I Lived in the state of Minnesota for seven months. Later on, I moved to Idaho. Since, I have grown roots in Idaho, and I
call Idaho home.

Living in Idaho for the past 13 years has allowed me to enjoy and appreciate the beauty of the outdoors. The beauty when
changing through the four seasons is much appreciated and enjoyed. I have to admit that witnessing those changes is one
of my favorite things to enjoy in life. Also, I have been able to start fun activities such as camping, fishing, hiking, riding
four-wheelers, and enjoying the art of riding a mountain bike going up and downhill.

I have learned to overcome challenges, such as learning a new language, adapting to a different culture, and accepting limitations.
Therefore, I have learned that many difficulties in life are not chosen, but other challenges can be chosen and all can be overcome.
That is my reason to get into the world of programming. I have decided that I can be successful and input my knowledge and
experience in the technology industry. An industry that only keeps growing, an industry that only helps me to keep growing.
