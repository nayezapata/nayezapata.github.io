About Us

Hello, Meet The World community. This is Nayeli Zapata, Meet The World Traveling Agency founder. I would like to tell you that I started Meet The World Traveling Agency 15 years ago. It all started because I was the person that worked from 9:00-to-5:00 and gave a certain amount of Vacation every year. That Vacation always seems to expire before I use it, and when I try to use it, it usually involves being expensive, stressed, and mentally tired. Being outdoors has always been of my favorite activities. It gives me a break from the daily stress and helps me to recharge my energy. Overall, it gives me the thrill of discovering something new in nature as well as showing me to have the love and respect that nature deserves. Therefore, I saw the need to appease my adventurous soul by traveling to natural destinations when my vacations were available. As I knew many others were feeling the same, I created this Agency to help other people with adventurous souls meet the world.

Why Us

Since I Founded Meet The World Agency, one of my commitments has always been empathizing with the adventurous souls out there. Therefore Meet The World Agency is constantly working hard to find the most adventurous packages and deals, so you, your family, and your friends can experience the world. Meet The World Agency believes in using your vacations for what it was meant for, which is to take a break from your daily routine and meet the world at the best deal possible.
